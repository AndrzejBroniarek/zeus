Welcome to the electronic voting booth by Zeus Elections.

At this point you must have followed the secret invitation sent to you by Email or SMS for the poll "{$T.election.full_name}".

To submit your vote follow the instructions. Your current progress and relevant information will be displayed, at the top of the page.

  1. Fill in ballot. In this step, you fill in the ballot with your choices.
  2. Ballot encryption and submission. The choices you made in the previous step will be encrypted in your own computer by the web browser itself. A submission receipt will be sent to your email address.

You can use your invitation to submit another vote. Each time, the new vote replaces your previous one.
